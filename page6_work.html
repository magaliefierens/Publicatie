ADVERTISING

The first assignment was an advertisement . We had to create a representation of a so-called 'non-place'. A place where we go to reach our destination, but is not the destination itself.

Designed May 2015
780 x 530mm

LP COVER

I chose for the album 'The 2nd Law' from Muse. It's one of my favorite bands. The meaning of 'The 2nd Law' is actually the second law of thermodynamics. Thermodynamics is a branch of physics concerned with heat and temperature and their relation to energy and work. So I associated it to a vulcano.

Designed February 2015
cover: 325 X 325 mm

LP BACKCOVER

For the back of the cover I used another association with thermodynamica. I used an image of a man who is welding.
In this work you also have that same reaction of producing heat.

Designed February 2015
backcover: 325 X 325 mm

LP SLEEVE

For the sleeve and the label of the LP I used the symbol of thermodynamics. I only used the lines of the symbol and inserted my own text in it.

Designed February 2015
sleeve: 305 X 305 mm
